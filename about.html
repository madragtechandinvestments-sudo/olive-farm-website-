About Our Farm

Olive Grove Farms is a family-owned olive farm dedicated to sustainable agriculture and traditional harvesting methods.

Our olives are carefully grown, hand-harvested, and cold-pressed to produce premium extra virgin olive oil known for its rich flavor and exceptional quality.

We value sustainability, integrity, and long-term partnerships with our customers and vendors.
